**1\. Foundations: Legal, Structure, & Financials**

To support a 50-state remote operation, your foundational setup must be legally compliant and financially lean.

### **Legal & Business Structuring**

* **Entity Selection:** Form an **LLC** initially for simplicity and asset protection, but elect **S-Corp status** through IRS Form 2553 once your net revenues clear roughly $80,000–$100,000 per founder. This will significantly optimize your self-employment tax burden.  
* **Multi-State Compliance:** Because you are placing candidates across all 50 states, ensure your client contracts state that the agreement is governed by the laws of your home state.  
* **Insurance (Non-Negotiable):** You need **Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions)** and **General Liability** insurance. Tech companies will require proof of insurance (COI) before signing your contract, especially if your placed candidates handle sensitive pre-sales data or client systems.

### **Client Agreement Blueprint**

Have a business attorney draft your standard **Master Services Agreement (MSA)**. Ensure these key clauses match your vision:

* **The 18% Introductory Clause:** State that the 18% fee applies *exclusively* to the first placement made within a 90-day window from signing, requiring a 14-day period of exclusivity. Subsequent hires revert to your standard tier (e.g., 25% for AEs).  
* **The 90-Day Guarantee:** Pro-rate this to protect yourselves. If a candidate leaves in Month 1, you replace them. If they leave on Day 89, you still replace them, but ensure you have a clause stating the client's account must be fully paid up to date for the guarantee to be valid.

Anonymize their profile and present them to a target VP of Sales at a 400-person AI company. *“I have a Senior AE who just finished at 150% of quota in the enterprise AI space and is looking for their next home. * **Referral Incentives:** Establish an elite referral network. Offer a high-end incentive (e.g., a $1,000 referral bonus paid out after the candidate clears their 90-day guarantee period) to top sales professionals who introduce you to peers in their network.
